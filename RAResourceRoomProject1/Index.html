Welcome to the Resident Assistant Resource Room!

Rather than wasting your time and your University's resources, order all of your floor community needs here!

Since working as a resident assistant at the University of Pittsburgh, I, and my fellow RA's, found that a general resource room for every RA was not the best solution.
With the current resource room situation, Resident Assistants...
  • Waste materials due to inexact cutting and counting of supplies
  • Clog the often small space with many other RA's
  • Do not clean up after themselves in the room
By using the online resource room, Resident Assistants...
  • Save materials and supplies
  • Save their University money
  • Do not have to make the trip to the resource room, supplies are mailed right to you
